Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this application handles information.

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not require user accounts and does not connect to external servers.

Any data entered into the app is stored locally on your device only (for example, calculator values or preferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking libraries, or third-party services that collect user data.

Network Usage

This app functions fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect any data from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
